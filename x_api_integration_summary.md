以下を申請フォームに貼り付ける想定。

```text
I will use the X API for my own account, yumin_ai, to support AI-related content publishing and workflow management.

The primary use cases are:

1. Draft review and publishing support
I will use the API to publish posts, replies, and threads from my own account after human review. Drafts may be generated or prepared in a separate workflow, but final posting decisions will be reviewed and approved by me.

2. Post management for my own account
I will retrieve and manage posts from my own account to maintain a content archive, check what has already been posted, avoid duplicate topics, and improve future posting workflows.

3. Basic engagement review
I may retrieve basic engagement information for my own posts, such as replies, reposts, likes, bookmarks, impressions, or similar metrics available through the API, to understand which AI-related topics are useful to my audience.

4. Content planning and analytics
I may use my own post history and engagement data to plan future posts about AI tools, Codex Automations, AI workflows, and practical AI usage. This analysis is for my own content operations only.

I will not resell X data or API outputs. I will not redistribute X Content outside what is permitted by X's Developer Agreement and Policy. I will not use X Content to train or fine-tune foundation models or frontier models. I will not create spam, automated unsolicited replies, mass engagement, or misleading activity. I will comply with X's Developer Agreement, Developer Policy, Automation Rules, Display Requirements, API Restricted Uses Rules, X Rules, and related policies.

The application is intended for limited personal or small-scale content operations for the account yumin_ai, not for a white-label service, resale of X data, surveillance, user profiling, or bulk data extraction.
```
